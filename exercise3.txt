1NF: no repeating groups, primary key defined 
	pt no*, full name, ward nr, ward name,  drug nr*, drug name, ddrug descr, drug dosage, method of admin, units per day, start date*, finish date

2NF: no partial dependencies
	patient(ptno*, full name, ward nr, ward name)
	drug(drug nr*, drug name, drug descr, drug dosage, method of admin)
	prescr(pt no*, drug nr*, units, start date*, finish date)
3NF: no transitive dependencies of non keys  
	ptno full name wardno bed
	ward no ward name 
	drug(as above)
	prescr(as above)

The database is in BCNF because, in every relation, all functional dependencies have a determinant that is a candidate key. 
There are no partial or transitive dependencies, 
and no non-key attribute determines another attribute.
